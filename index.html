Our Story

A Family Tradition,
Rooted in Heritage

Grateful Bread began as an independent bakery and café in Seattle's Wedgwood neighborhood in 1996, founded by Rod and Judy Neldam. What started as a neighborhood staple grew into something beloved — and in 2014, they brought that same warmth and craftsmanship to the heart of Duvall, WA.

Rod is 3rd generation Danish, and some of Grateful Bread's recipes trace all the way back to Neldam's Danish Bakery in Oakland, California — a legendary institution founded in 1929. That heritage lives in every loaf, every pastry, every bite.

Every item is made from scratch with zero preservatives, no GMOs, and no corn syrup. Just real, local, organic ingredients — baked fresh every single day.
